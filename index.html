About me

I hold a BSc and PhD from the Department of Informatics, Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, Greece. My thesis was on Supervised and Unsupervised Deep Learning Methodologies, focusing on Robotic Vision tasks as well as timeseries analysis, mainly concerning Gravitational Wave data. I've also been actively involved in multiple research projects, ranging from robotics to financial and energy-load forecasting.

I am currently working as a Post-Doctoral Researcher at the Computational Intelligence and Deep Learning Lab at Aristotle University of Thessaloniki.

Projects

OpenDR [website] [github]

Developing an open, modular, non-proprietary toolkit for core robotic capabilities. My contribution pertains to Deep Learning-based 2D image analysis tools, like object recognition, detection and tracking, both human-centric and environmental. Developed tools in PyTorch and MXNet and corresponding ROS nodes.

DeepFinance [website]

R&D in Deep Learning for portfolio management, based on financial timeseries analysis as well as semantic analysis of social media posts related to the financial assets at hand. My role: development of relevant models and management of the project.

MALENA [website]

Electrical load demand forecasting using Deep Learning. My role: researching DL-based regression methods for the ELDF problem.

Virgo-AUTH

I've been a member of the Virgo-AUTH team since 2019, researching DL-based methods for improved surrogate waveform modelling, as well as Gravitational Wave detection in real noise, competing in the first Machine Learning Mock Data Challenge [MLGWSC-1]. Our model [AResGW] outperformed other ML-based methods in real noise from O3a, and an improved version [arXiv] further decreased the gap between ML and matched filtering based methods.

MultiDrone [website]

Developing a multi-drone system for autonomous sports-related cinematography. My research was focused on lightweight Deep Learning methods for robotic vision, as the drones were equipped with NVIDIA Jetson TX2 modules. I developed ROS nodes for joint detection-tracking after researching a method for long-term classification-based redetection tracking [paper].
